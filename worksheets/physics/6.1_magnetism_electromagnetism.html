Mark Scheme

1. Like poles repel, unlike attract; field lines show direction (N to S) and strength (spacing) [3]
2. Hard materials retain magnetism (permanent magnets); soft materials easily magnetised/demagnetised (electromagnets) [3]
3. Field is circular around wire; solenoid gives uniform field inside like a bar magnet [3]
4. Motor effect: force on current in magnetic field; left-hand rule relates field, current, force [3]
5. Move coil/conductor through field; change magnetic field through coil [2]
6. Relative motion between coil and magnet induces voltage; increase speed, number of turns, magnetic field strength [4]
