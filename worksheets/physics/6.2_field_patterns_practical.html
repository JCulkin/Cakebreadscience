6.6-6.7 Field Patterns
Key Concepts: Field patterns can be mapped with iron filings or a plotting compass. Two magnets can create a uniform field.

Section A: Practical Method

1. Describe how to use iron filings to show a magnetic field pattern around a bar magnet. [3]
2. Describe how to use a plotting compass to map a magnetic field. [3]
3. Explain how to create a near-uniform magnetic field using two magnets. [2]
Total marks: 8

Mark Scheme

1. Place paper over magnet, sprinkle filings, tap gently to show lines [3]
2. Move compass step by step, mark direction, join to show lines [3]
3. Place unlike poles facing with small gap for uniform field region [2]
