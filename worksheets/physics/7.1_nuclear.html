IGCSE Edexcel Physics
7.1–7.26 Radioactivity, Fission and Fusion
Key Concepts: Radioactivity is the spontaneous emission of particles from unstable nuclei. Alpha particles (He nuclei), beta particles (electrons), and gamma rays are three types of radiation. Half-life is the time for half a sample to decay. Nuclear reactions involve mass-energy conversion via E = mc².
