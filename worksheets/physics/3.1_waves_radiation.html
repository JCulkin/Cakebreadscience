Waves and the Electromagnetic Spectrum

IGCSE Edexcel Physics
3.1–3.13 Waves and EM Spectrum
Key Concepts: Waves transfer energy without transferring matter. Key properties include wavelength (λ), frequency (f), and amplitude (A). Wave equation: v = fλ. The electromagnetic spectrum includes radio, microwaves, infrared, visible light, ultraviolet, X-rays, and gamma rays. Sound waves are mechanical waves requiring a medium.
