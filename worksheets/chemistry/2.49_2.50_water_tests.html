2.49-2.50 Tests for water and purity
Key Concept: Anhydrous copper(II) sulfate and cobalt chloride paper detect water. Pure water has a sharp boiling point.

Section A: Tests for Water

1. Describe the test for water using anhydrous copper(II) sulfate. [2]
2. Describe the test for water using cobalt chloride paper. [2]

Section B: Test for Purity

3. How can you show that a sample of water is pure using a physical test? [2]
4. A sample boils over a range of temperatures. What does this suggest? [1]

Total marks: 7

Mark Scheme

1. White anhydrous CuSO4 turns blue in presence of water [2]
